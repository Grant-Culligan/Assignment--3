Computer Engineering

Computer Engineers evaluate, test, maintain computer hardware and software. They develop, design new computer parts, like circuitboards, network systems, computer processors. Computer Engineers help ressolve problems/address any concerns to due with hardware/software related features; aid in the advancement computer technology. The responsibilities of a computer engineer include, desinging and testing robots, researching software and computer hardware, writing and testing software for devices, managing operating systems, testing hardware components like processors, circuit boards.
Most positions of computer engineering employers require a bachelor's degree in computer science, some Computer Engineers major in mathmatics, software engineering or electrical engineering.
The career outlook for Computer Engineers is expected to grow about 5% from 2016-2026.
The average salary for a Canadian based computer engineer is 74,969/year as of April 30, 2021.
